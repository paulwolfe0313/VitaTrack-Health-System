About VitaTrack Health System

VitaTrack Health System was founded in 2024 by Dr. George Jacobs and Dr. Phil Donaldson, two passionate healthcare professionals who recognized the need for a more efficient way to manage patient information in medical facilities. Both doctors had experienced firsthand the challenges and inefficiencies of paper-based record-keeping systems and outdated software solutions.

Determined to make a difference, Dr. Jacobs and Dr. Donaldson embarked on a journey to develop a comprehensive electronic health record (EHR) system that would revolutionize the healthcare industry. After years of research, development, and collaboration with other healthcare professionals, VitaTrack Health System was born.

Our mission at VitaTrack Health System is to empower healthcare providers with innovative technology solutions that streamline the process of managing patient information. We believe that by providing healthcare professionals with easy access to accurate and up-to-date patient records, we can improve patient care, enhance efficiency, and ultimately save lives.

Today, VitaTrack Health System is proud to serve medical facilities of all sizes, from small clinics to large hospitals, across the globe. We remain committed to our mission of transforming healthcare through technology and continue to innovate and evolve to meet the ever-changing needs of the medical field.
